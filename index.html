Somerville Bag Homepage

Passionate about Ultimate and in Boston this summer? You've come to the right place.

Somerville Bag is a local club Ultimate organization dedicated to providing mediocre content for those pursing the great sport of Ultimate Frisbee. Founded in 20XX by Tufts students, Somerville Bag partners with the Tufts EMEN and USAU to provide oppportunities for players looking to follow their Ultimate passion in club. Disclaimer: Bag bag Bag.
